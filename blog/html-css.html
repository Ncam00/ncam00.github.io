Margin, Border, and Padding

The diffenence between margins, borders and padding. Is that padding creates space between the element's content and its border, border is the visible or invisible line around the padding and content and margin creates space outside the border, separating the element from other elements on the page.

  • Padding adds space between the content of an element and its border.
  • Border is the line (visible or not) that wraps around the padding and content.
  • Margin adds space outside the border, separating the element from others around it on the page.
