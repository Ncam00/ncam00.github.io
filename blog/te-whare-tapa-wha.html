Te Whare Tapa Wha


Te Whare Tapa Wha, consist of 4 dimensions


    taha tinana (physical wellbing)
    taha hinengaro (mental wellbeing)
    taha wairua (spritual wellbeing)
    taha whānau (family wellbeing)
    whenua

Taha Tinana is your physical wellbeing - It's how you grow as an invividual, how you move, how you feel and take care for yourself


Taha Hinengaro is your mental wellbeing - It's your feelings, thoughts and you express them. It's about how you think, communicate with others and how you feel.


Taha Wairua is your spritual wellbeing - It's beliefs or religion. These are normally expressed through practices and traditions.


Tana Whānau is your family wellbeing - It's who cares about you and who you can share life with. This could be immediate family, extended family or partner.

Whenua, this is the 4 dimensions to the foundation - The health of the land and the natural environment is strongly connected to your health and wellbeing.
